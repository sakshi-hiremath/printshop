Our Mission

Say Goodbye to Exam-Season Chaos

During lab submissions and exam prep, college print shops turn into overcrowded war zones. Students waste hours in queues while CRs struggle to manage manual lists and cash collections.

PrintShop revolutionizes the process: CRs launch timed polls with UPI QR codes, students join digitally, upload payment proofs, and the system auto-generates a comparison list. No more confusion, no more waiting — just efficient bulk printing.

Simple 4-Step Workflow

From poll creation to bulk printing — everything streamlined

1

CR Creates Poll

Set subject, price/copy, upload UPI QR code, and launch with 1-hour expiry timer.

2

Students Join & Pay

View active polls, pay via UPI, upload screenshot proof — all within the poll window.

3

Comparison List

System auto-generates verified vs pending list. CR verifies screenshots with one click.

4

Bulk Print

CR heads to shop with exact copy count, total collected amount — prints in one go.

Formula: Total Collected = Verified Payments × Price Per Copy No guesswork, exact cash handover
